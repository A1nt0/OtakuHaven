  • 1. Kuroko no Basket

    Watch on: Crunchyroll, Netflix

    Kuroko Tetsuya, the phantom sixth man, teams up with Kagami Taiga to take on the Generation of Miracles and become the best in Japanese high school basketball.

  • 2. JoJo's Bizarre Adventure

    Watch on: Crunchyroll, Netflix

    The Joestar family, across multiple generations, fights against supernatural foes using their unique abilities and stands in this epic saga.

  • 3. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

    Watch on: Crunchyroll, Netflix

    Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy, joins the Demon Slayer Corps to avenge his family and cure his sister Nezuko, who was turned into a demon.

  • 4. Attack on Titan

    Watch on: Crunchyroll, Netflix

    In a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction, Eren Yeager and his friends join the fight against the terrifying Titans, who threaten their existence.

  • 5. The Seven Deadly Sins

    Watch on: Netflix

    Elizabeth, the princess of Liones, searches for the Seven Deadly Sins, a group of knights, to help reclaim her kingdom from the Holy Knights.

  • 6. Black Clover

    Watch on: Crunchyroll, Funimation

    Asta, a boy born without magic in a world where magic is everything, aims to become the Wizard King with the help of his friends and his anti-magic sword.

  • 7. Hunter x Hunter

    Watch on: Crunchyroll, Netflix

    Gon Freecss, a young boy with incredible potential, sets out to become a Hunter and find his father, meeting friends and overcoming challenges along the way.
