Where to Watch Popular Animes

  • Attack on Titan

    Watch on: Crunchyroll, Netflix

    In a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction, Eren Yeager and his friends join the fight against the terrifying Titans, who threaten their existence.

  • My Hero Academia

    Watch on: Crunchyroll, Funimation

    In a world where almost everyone has superpowers, young Izuku Midoriya dreams of becoming a hero despite being born powerless.

  • Demon Slayer

    Watch on: Crunchyroll, Netflix

    Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy, joins the Demon Slayer Corps to avenge his family and cure his sister Nezuko, who was turned into a demon.

  • Jujutsu Kaisen

    Watch on: Crunchyroll, Funimation

    Yuji Itadori, a high school student with incredible strength, becomes a Jujutsu Sorcerer to combat curses and save innocent lives after ingesting a powerful curse.

  • One Piece

    Watch on: Crunchyroll, Netflix

    Monkey D. Luffy sets sail with his crew to find the One Piece, the greatest treasure in the world, and become the Pirate King.

  • Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

    Watch on: Netflix, Hulu

    Brothers Edward and Alphonse Elric use alchemy in their quest to find the Philosopher's Stone and restore their bodies after a failed transmutation.

  • Tokyo Revengers

    Watch on: Crunchyroll

    Takemichi Hanagaki travels back in time to his middle school days to save his ex-girlfriend from being murdered by the Tokyo Manji Gang.

Top 5 Best Animes to Watch in 2024

  • 1. Attack on Titan: The Final Season

    Watch on: Crunchyroll

    The gripping conclusion to Eren Yeager's journey as he battles the Titans and faces shocking truths about the world and himself.

  • 2. Jujutsu Kaisen

    Watch on: Crunchyroll

    Yuji Itadori continues his fight against curses alongside his friends and mentors at the Tokyo Metropolitan Magic Technical College.

  • 3. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

    Watch on: Crunchyroll

    Tanjiro Kamado's relentless quest to defeat powerful demons and protect his sister Nezuko reaches new heights.

  • 4. My Hero Academia

    Watch on: Crunchyroll

    Izuku Midoriya and his classmates at U.A. High School continue their journey to become pro heroes while facing new villains.

  • 5. One Piece

    Watch on: Crunchyroll

    The Straw Hat Pirates' adventures continue as they explore new lands, face powerful foes, and seek the legendary One Piece treasure.
